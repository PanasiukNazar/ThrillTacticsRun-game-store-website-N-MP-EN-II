About Our Store

Our Story

Thrill Tactics Run Shop started with a clear goal: to provide the ultimate destination for digital and physical PC and console games. Built by gamers, for gamers, we've grown into a trusted name in the gaming world.

From popular AAA blockbusters to hidden indie gems, we curate a collection that meets the needs of casual players and competitive gamers alike. We’re passionate about helping every visitor discover their next favorite game.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to redefine how people access and enjoy video games. We’re committed to offering instant digital key delivery, fast service, and a shopping experience that’s simple and secure.

We aim to create a platform where gamers can find exactly what they’re looking for — and discover even more. As gaming continues to evolve, so will we, always focused on innovation and community satisfaction.

How We Grew

Founded by Alex Moore and David Smith, what began as a passion project quickly turned into a growing store trusted by gamers around the world. We focused on making great games more accessible and delivery lightning-fast.

Our customers’ feedback helped us fine-tune our website, expand our library, and add features gamers actually want. Every step forward has been inspired by the gaming community we proudly serve.

Why Choose Thrill Tactics Run

We offer one of the largest selections of PC and console games online, with titles for PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo, and more. Our team is dedicated to customer satisfaction, fast delivery, and easy navigation.

With pre-order access, top sellers, bundles, and seasonal sales, we bring unbeatable deals to your fingertips. Whether you're into strategy, shooters, or story-driven adventures, we’re here to power your next gaming session.
